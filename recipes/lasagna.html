Lazy Lasagna

Description

Today I'm going to teach you how to make a lasagna is to die for! On top of that it's so easy, even your cats could whip it up! Let's get started, an easy to follow guide with measured ingredients is below!

Step by Step

  1. Mix your ricotta, mozzarella, and parmesan blend and refrigerate
  2. Pre heat your oven to 350Â° and set a pot of water to boil
  3. Once the water is boiling add in the lasagna noodles
  4. When the noodles are al dente, remove them from the water and set aside
  5. Season and brown ground beef in a skillet over high heat
  6. Once meat is cooked add in one can of your preferred pasta sauce
  7. Cook pasta sauce until warmed throughout and slightly thickened
  8. Now take your ingredients and layer them in a cake pan in order: sauce, noodles, cheese mix, so on
  9. Once you've filled the pan set it in the oven's middle rack for 30 minutes
  10. After 30 minutes have passed, throw some extra cheese on top for a nice crust and pop it back in for 15 minutes
  11. Remove from the oven, let it cool for 5 minutes, and serve!

Ingredients

  • 1 tub ricotta cheese
  • A few handfuls of shredded mozarella
  • A gracious amount of parmesan
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 box lasagna noodles
  • 1 can of pasta sauce
