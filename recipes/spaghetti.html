Simple Spaghetti

Description

We've got an easy spaghetti recipe for you today! As if we couldn't make it easier, we only have 3 ingredients and 7 steps for this one. Sit down and relax because this one basically cooks itself!

Step by Step

  1. Get a pot of water boiling on the stove top
  2. Once the water is boiling, drop in your noodles and stir
  3. Season and brown the ground beef to your preference
  4. Add the can of pasta sauce to your beef and season to taste
  5. Bring sauce mix to a boil, stirring occasionally, and then remove from heat
  6. Once noodles are al dente, strain the water
  7. Plate and serve!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 can pasta sauce
  • 1 box pasta noodles
